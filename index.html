Bal Shakti Puraskar

Raghav Puri Won Bal Shakti Puraskar

It is matter of immense pride that Raghav Puri, a Class XII student of DPS Dwarka, was awarded the Pradhan Mantri Rashtriya Bal Shakti Puraskar 2020 by the Hon’ble President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind and was also felicitated by the Hon’ble Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi. He was awarded in the field of innovation for his project 'Recovering History'. He has made incredible technological interventions through the project ‘Recovering History’ which uses Artificial Intelligence to reconstruct broken parts of artifacts and manuscripts for better study of history and culture. He represented India with this project at the International Science and Engineering Fair (ISEF) held at Arizona. This is the world’s biggest science fair with participants from more than 70 countries.

The ceremony took place virtually on 12 November,2020. The Student Council was appointed for the primary wing. It started with a virtual lighting of the lamp then a talk show and then the levitation started. Headmistress mam gave the vote of thanks. It was a pleasure to celebrate this ceremony.




Mélange 2020

With an aim to foster creative thinking and simultaneously nurture the competitive streak amongst students, DPS Dwarka organised an Inter-School Event, Mélange 2020, for the students of classes Pre-School to V, from Thursday, 19 November to Saturday, 5 December 2020, through the virtual mode. A plethora of innovative and interesting competitions catering to the literary and cultural fields were held to harness the inherent potential of the students in diverse domains. The event, Mélange 2020, a cornucopia of literary and cultural competitions, witnessed an enthusiastic participation from 20 schools under various categories. It served as a unique platform to enhance knowledge, develop personality and shape perspectives of the students in a competitive setup.




Nihon No Kaori 2019

DPS Dwarka under the aegis of the DPS Society organized Nihon No Kaori 2019 – Annual Inter DPS Japanese Language Festival on Friday, 23 August 2019. Students from twelve Delhi Public Schools participated in various offsite and onsite events. The Valedictory Ceremony was graced by Ms. Namita Pradhan, Chairperson, DPS Dwarka as the Chief Guest. Dr. Zeenat Khader, Joint Secretary, DPS Society was the Guest of Honour. The event was also graced by Mr. Masahiro Kobayashi, First Secretary, Embassy of Japan as the Distinguished Guest.
